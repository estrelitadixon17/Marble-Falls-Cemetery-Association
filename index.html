About Our Association

A Legacy of Respect in Texas

Founded on the principles of community, respect, and historical preservation, the Marble Falls Cemetery Association serves as the steward of our community's most sacred grounds in Marble Falls, Texas. Our mission is to provide a serene, meticulously maintained environment where families can peacefully visit and remember their loved ones.

As a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, we operate entirely through the dedication of our volunteers, board members, and the generous tax-deductible contributions of families and local supporters. Every dollar donated goes directly toward the landscaping, infrastructure maintenance, and long-term preservation of the cemetery.

We believe that a well-cared-for cemetery is a reflection of a community's soul. By maintaining these grounds, we are not just caring for the past; we are providing a beautiful, comforting space for the generations of today and tomorrow.

Our Commitments

Grounds Maintenance

We ensure the grass is mowed, trees are pruned, and pathways are cleared year-round, providing a dignified and safe environment for all visitors.

Historical Preservation

We carefully monitor and restore aging monuments, ensuring the rich history of Marble Falls and its founding families is never lost to time.

Genealogy & Records

We maintain accurate maps and interment records to assist families and historians in locating gravesites and tracing their family lineage.

Rules & Policies

Cemetery Rules & Regulations

To maintain the solemnity and beauty of the grounds, we ask all visitors to adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Visiting Hours: The cemetery is open to the public from dawn until dusk. Nighttime visitation is strictly prohibited.
  • Floral Arrangements: Fresh cut flowers are encouraged. Artificial flowers are permitted but may be removed by groundskeepers when they become faded or weathered. Glass containers are strictly prohibited for safety reasons.
  • Decorations: Fences, borders, solar lights, and excessive ornaments around individual gravesites impede mowing and maintenance. The Association reserves the right to remove non-compliant items.
  • Pets: Pets must be leashed at all times, and owners must clean up after them.

Privacy Policy

The Marble Falls Cemetery Association respects your privacy. Any personal information collected through inquiries, plot purchases, or donations (including names, addresses, and email addresses) is used strictly for internal record-keeping and communication. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties unless required by law.
