Our Mission

A 501(c)(3) Nonprofit Organization

The Marble Falls Cemetery Association is dedicated to the perpetual care, maintenance, and historical preservation of our community’s final resting places. Through the generosity of donors and the dedication of our volunteers, we ensure that every monument tells a story and every family find peace within our gates.

Tracing Our Roots

Established during the founding years of Marble Falls, our cemetery serves as a silent chronicle of the pioneers, leaders, and families who built this region. From the granite-carved markers of the 1800s to the modern memorial gardens of today, we provide a bridge between generations.

As a non-profit association, we operate independently of the municipal government. This means our beauty and stability depend entirely on the support of our community. We handle landscaping, security, historical documentation, and infrastructure improvements.

  • Official State Historical Markers on site.
  • Veterans Memorial Row honoring local heroes.
  • Rare flora and century-old oak preservation.
